Our automated features

Lead Generation

Landing Page Automation: Create high-converting landing pages with built-in lead capture forms. These pages can be customized for different campaigns and automatically funnel data into CRM systems.
Lead Scoring: Automatically score leads based on predefined criteria (such as behavior, demographics, or engagement) to prioritize high-quality prospects.
Lead Capture: Use chatbots or pop-up forms on websites to engage visitors, capture leads, and provide instant responses or incentives.

Email Marketing

Automated Email Sequences: Set up series of emails (welcome emails, nurturing emails, product recommendations, etc.) that are triggered based on user actions or timelines.
Segmentation & Personalization: Automatically segment email lists based on customer behavior, location, or interests. Emails can be personalized with dynamic content.
Drip Campaigns: Trigger a series of automated emails based on user actions such as signing up for a newsletter or downloading a resource.

Reporting & Analytics

Automated Reporting Dashboards: Tools like Google Data Studio, HubSpot, and Google Analytics allow you to create custom, real-time reporting dashboards that automatically pull in data from multiple sources and present it in a digestible format.
Scheduled Reports: Automate the generation and delivery of reports to clients on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. These can include metrics like ROI, customer acquisition costs, conversion rates, and social media performance.

Social Media Scheduling

Scheduled Posting: Automate posts across multiple platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter) by scheduling content in advance using tools like Buffer, Hootsuite, or Sprout Social.
Content Curation: Use automated content curation tools to find and schedule relevant content that will engage your audience based on their interests.

Consultancy

Strategic Planning Developing long-term marketing strategies to meet business goals. This can involve market research, competitor analysis, and defining target audiences.
Branding and Positioning Helping companies develop a strong, distinctive brand that resonates with their target audience, and positioning their brand effectively in the marketplace.

Client Onboarding

Automated Welcome Emails: Send personalized welcome emails to new clients, introducing your agency and explaining the next steps.
Onboarding Forms: Automate the process of gathering necessary client information.
Project Setup: Automatically create new projects, assign tasks, and set milestones when a new client signs up.
